About

AI Artathon is one of Global AI Summit initiatives to be held in Riyadh on 23 - 25 January 2020. It is the first art based on artificial intelligence artathon competition, to create awareness about artficial intelligence throught art.

Objective

The unique program teaming up artists, and AI experts with the objective of producing 10 AI artworks, and showcase them in an exhibition part of the Global AI Summit 2020
