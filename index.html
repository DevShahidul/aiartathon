About

AI Artathon is one of Global AI Summit initiatives to be held in Riyadh on 23 - 25 January 2020. It is the first art based on artificial intelligence artathon competition, to create awareness about artficial intelligence throught art.

Objective

The unique program teaming up artists, and AI experts with the objective of producing 10 AI artworks, and showcase them in an exhibition part of the Global AI Summit 2020

Artathon Goals

Why Participate?

  • Get a unique opportunity to learn new AI art skills such as data science, visual arts and product design.
  • Engage with like - minded people and be part of an active community of AI Art enthusiasts
  • Get the chance to be Scouted by government and private sector organizations.
  • Showcase your AI artworks in Global AI Summit to international AI experts audience

Create awareness about AI through art

Engage the public domain with AI through art and culture an interactive manner

Encourage the innovation in AI

Promote the field of AI by taking developers, programmers & various type of visual art communities on an artistic journey

Nurture and empower AI communities

Create a platform for AI enthusiasts to engage and collaborate on multi - fronts to foster an ever - growing community of new generation of artist

Important registration details

  • Teams which are more diverse in terms of their functionas will be preferred
  • Registration does not necessarily mean acceptance. Accepted participants will receive confirmation emails.
  • Participant screening and slection will be based on criteria such as, registraion as a team, the diversity of team members, and the individual skill set of a participant
  • Individuals will be given time to form a team on the first day of the event
